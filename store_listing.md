## Full Description
Transform your life one habit at a time with Habitly, the habit tracker that makes building positive routines fun and rewarding.

Habitly combines streak tracking, visual progress charts, and celebration animations to keep you motivated on your journey to self-improvement. Whether you want to exercise daily, read more, drink water, or meditate, Habitly helps you stay consistent.

**Key Features:**

- **Streak Tracking** - Build momentum with satisfying streak counters that motivate consistency
- **Beautiful Progress Charts** - Visualize your habit data with stunning FL Chart analytics
- **Celebration Animations** - Confetti and Lottie animations reward your achievements
- **Smart Reminders** - Local notifications ensure you never forget your habits
- **Haptic Feedback** - Satisfying vibrations when you check off completed habits
- **Flexible Scheduling** - Set habits for daily, weekly, or custom schedules
- **Habit Categories** - Organize habits by health, productivity, mindfulness, and more
- **Detailed Statistics** - Track completion rates, best streaks, and monthly progress
- **Dark & Light Themes** - Comfortable viewing in any environment

Habitly is designed with behavioral psychology principles to help habits stick. The reward system gives your brain the positive reinforcement it needs to turn actions into automatic routines.

No sign-ups. No subscriptions. No ads. Just a beautiful tool to become the best version of yourself.
